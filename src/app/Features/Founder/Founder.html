Meet The Founder

My name is Bashir Alshihabi, founder of “Chehabi Academy” and language teacher. I was born in 1984 in Damascus, Syria and I belong to a poet family which has been concerned with literature and poetry for more than 300 years. I am also a poet and a musician. I studied Guitar and Music Theory in Damascus, Stuttgart and Vienna.

In Germany I gave many Arabic and German language courses and translated and interpreted at different schools, universities, companies and agencies such as:
  • Volkshochschule Stuttgart
  • Volkshochschule Ludwigsburg
  • Volkshochschule Leonberg
  • Pädagogische Hochschule Ludwigsburg
  • Robert Bosch GmbH, Stuttgart
  • Stiftung Karlshöhe Ludwigsburg
  • Jugendamt Stuttgart
  • Polizeipräsidium Stuttgart
